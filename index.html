About Us

We are the leading name in professional trucking and reliable transportation services since 1998. With over 25 years of operation in the GCC region, we have built a solid reputation for excellence and dependability.We specialize in a wide range of transportation services, including heavy haulage, breakbulk, and project cargo movements.

Our fleet of over 100 units is equipped to handle a variety of transportation needs, from inland logistics to heavy lift operations. Whether you require low bed trailers, extra-long trailers up to 35 meters, tipper trucks, curtain trailers, box trailers, fuel tankers, light trucks, or heavy flatbeds with capacities of up to 100 tons, we have the equipment and expertise to get the job done efficiently and safely.

At Arabian Sky Transport, we pride ourselves on our commitment to customer satisfaction and our ability to deliver solutions tailored to meet your specific requirements. With a team of experienced professionals and a dedication to excellence, we are your trusted partner for oversized cargo transportation in the United Arab Emirates. Experience the difference with Arabian Sky Transport – your reliable partner for all your transportation needs.
